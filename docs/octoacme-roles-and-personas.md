# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers shape the user experience through research, design, and usability testing. They translate requirements into intuitive user flows and collaborate with developers and product managers to ensure solutions are both functional and user-friendly.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and translate findings into design specifications
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and design systems
- Lead usability testing sessions and iterate based on feedback
- Translate product requirements into user flows and interaction patterns
- Provide input during planning and review stages to ensure usability
- Collaborate on accessibility and design standards

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, user-centric experiences that drive adoption and satisfaction
- Reduce support burden through clear, discoverable interfaces
- Build consistent design patterns across the product

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and feedback sessions
- User research summaries and insights
- Collaboration on acceptance criteria involving UX
- Design system and component documentation

### Interactions
- **Product Managers**: Align on feature requirements and user needs
- **Developers**: Partner on implementation feasibility and technical constraints
- **Project Managers**: Coordinate design timelines and resource planning
- **QA/Testing**: Review acceptance criteria for UI/UX elements

---

## Technical Writer

### Role Summary
Technical Writers create and maintain clear, accessible documentation that helps users understand and use the product effectively. They ensure consistency across all documentation and collaborate with developers and product teams.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain user documentation, developer guides, and API references
- Draft release notes and migration guides
- Develop onboarding materials and knowledge base articles
- Review and improve clarity of existing documentation
- Ensure technical accuracy and consistency across all written materials
- Incorporate feedback from support and product teams

### Goals
- Reduce support queries through clear, comprehensive documentation
- Accelerate developer and user onboarding
- Maintain a single source of truth for product knowledge

### Typical Communication
- Documentation reviews with subject matter experts
- Collaboration with developers on API and feature documentation
- Sync with Product Managers on release notes and feature announcements
- Feedback from support and customer success teams

### Interactions
- **Developers**: Partner on technical accuracy and implementation details
- **Project Managers**: Align on documentation timelines and release schedules
- **Product Managers**: Stay informed of feature announcements and changes
- **Customer Support**: Incorporate user questions and common issues into docs
- **QA/Testing**: Validate step-by-step instructions and procedures

---

## DevOps / Site Reliability Engineer (SRE)

### Role Summary
DevOps and SRE roles ensure smooth operation of CI/CD pipelines, infrastructure, and production systems. They automate deployments, monitor system health, and respond to operational incidents to maintain reliability and performance.

### Responsibilities
- Build and maintain CI/CD pipelines and automation
- Manage cloud infrastructure and deployment environments
- Monitor application health, performance, and error rates
- Automate infrastructure provisioning and scaling
- Develop runbooks and playbooks for common operational scenarios
- Lead incident response and post-incident reviews
- Ensure security best practices in infrastructure and deployments

### Goals
- Achieve high system reliability and uptime
- Reduce manual deployment effort and human error
- Enable rapid, safe deployments to production
- Minimize incident response time and impact

### Typical Communication
- Deployment coordination and release readiness reviews
- On-call escalations and incident notifications
- Infrastructure and deployment documentation
- Collaboration on reliability and performance metrics

### Interactions
- **Developers**: Coordinate on deployment requirements and CI/CD improvements
- **Project Managers**: Align on deployment schedules and production readiness
- **QA/Testing**: Partner on staging environment setup and smoke test infrastructure
- **Security**: Ensure security scanning and compliance in CI/CD pipelines

---

## Data Analyst / Data Scientist

### Role Summary
Data Analysts and Scientists use data to inform decisions, measure impact, and identify opportunities. They design tracking systems, analyze product metrics, and provide insights that drive product and business improvements.

### Responsibilities
- Define success metrics and tracking requirements during planning
- Design event schemas and instrumentation with developers
- Analyze product usage, performance, and user behavior
- Create dashboards and reports for stakeholders and product teams
- Identify trends, opportunities, and potential issues in data
- Support A/B testing and experimentation frameworks
- Provide data-driven recommendations for product and process improvements

### Goals
- Enable fact-based decision-making across the organization
- Quickly identify product opportunities and risks through data
- Support continuous iteration based on evidence

### Typical Communication
- Weekly metrics reviews and insights presentations
- Collaboration on metric definitions and tracking requirements
- Dashboard and report sharing with stakeholders
- Data findings incorporated into retrospectives and planning

### Interactions
- **Product Managers**: Define success metrics and interpret findings for prioritization
- **Developers**: Partner on event instrumentation and technical data collection
- **Project Managers**: Provide data on project outcomes and process efficiency
- **Stakeholders**: Present insights and data-driven recommendations
- **QA/Testing**: Support quality metrics and testing data analysis

---

## Customer Support / Success

### Role Summary
Support and Success teams are the voice of the customer. They provide direct user feedback, document support requests and trends, and collaborate with product and engineering teams to inform priorities and improvements.

### Responsibilities
- Provide first-line support and triage customer issues
- Document common customer questions, pain points, and feature requests
- Identify trends in support tickets and communicate to product team
- Contribute to knowledge base and help resources
- Support QA testing by providing real-world usage scenarios
- Participate in retrospectives to share customer feedback
- Help with rollout planning and customer communication for releases

### Goals
- Maximize customer satisfaction and retention
- Reduce support volume through better products and documentation
- Bridge the gap between customers and the product team

### Typical Communication
- Customer feedback summaries and trend reports
- Support ticket insights shared in retrospectives and planning
- Collaboration with product team on prioritization
- Coordination with developers on bug fixes and workarounds

### Interactions
- **Product Managers**: Communicate customer needs and priorities
- **Developers**: Report bugs, request features, provide usage context
- **Project Managers**: Share timelines for fixes and features with customers
- **Technical Writers**: Contribute customer questions to documentation
- **QA/Testing**: Provide real-world test scenarios and edge cases
